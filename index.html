Introduction

As technology becomes more advanced, artificial intelligence has become essential in simulating real-life human thoughts and capabilities. ChatGPT has made it possible for people to interact with machines by giving out responses based on user prompts. While impressive, ChatGPT is able to perform complex tasks such as writing emails, resume applications, and business plans with exceptional accuracy.

Here’s some of the features you can use with ChatGPT: - Add photos & files (look into images or documents to answer prompt) - Thinking (gives some more thought on prompt) - Deep Research (even further thought on prompt) - Web Search (search current web information based on prompt) - Canvas (creates a digital workspace based on prompt) - Quizzes (provides questions and tests on prompt)
