Introduction

As technology becomes more advanced, artificial intelligence has become essential in simulating real-life human thoughts and capabilities. ChatGPT has made it possible for people to interact with machines by giving out responses based on user prompts. While impressive, ChatGPT is able to perform complex tasks such as writing emails, resume applications, and business plans with exceptional accuracy.

Here’s some of the features you can use with ChatGPT:

  • Add Photos & Files (look into images or documents to answer prompt)
  • Thinking (gives some more thought on prompt)
  • Deep Research (even further thought on prompt)
  • Web Search (search current web information based on prompt)
  • Canvas (creates a digital workspace based on prompt)
  • Quizzes (provides questions and tests on prompt)

    • History

    ChatGPT was initially created by a non-profit company, OpenAI, to create technological benefits for humanity. After multiple iterations of its Large Language Models (LLM) to process large amounts of data across the internet and heavy machine learning for the AI to be knowledgeable in decision making, the chatbot became widely adopted for many productivity tasks. Humans have limits when it comes to jobs because each person has specialized knowledge and certain skillsets based on their background. ChatGPT, however, presented an opportunity to change the present norm. Generative Pretrained Transformers (GPT) are commonly used in ChatGPT systems which act like the brains for the AI and consistent online information makes the AI become smarter on any given topic because it can recognize patterns while reinforcing that behavior into their mind after receiving feedback. If ChatGPT receives enough educational resources, it has the potential to provide expert advice on things like finance and healthcare simultaneously. ChatGPT has received many upgrades for the past couple of years and OpenAI started introducing free and subscription based GPT models due to high demand. Free tier provides a ChatGPT model made to handle basic tasks but has several limits on sustaining long conversations with limited features but subscription tier offers the most advanced AI with no restrictions.
