Recommendations to Stakeholders

The future of ChatGPT is dependent on what steps are taken next. By providing thoughtful recommendations, the creators of ChatGPT can use that feedback to improve its technological integration towards people.

Students and General Users

Users should treat ChatGPT as a support tool, not a replacement for their own thinking. They should fact-check important information and avoid sharing private or sensitive details.

Educators

Educators should create clear AI-use policies and teach students how to use ChatGPT ethically for brainstorming, studying, and improving understanding rather than copying full answers.

Businesses

Businesses should use ChatGPT to improve customer service while keeping human review available for complex or serious issues. They should also be transparent when customers are interacting with AI.

Developers and Policymakers

AI developers and policymakers should support stronger privacy protections, third-party audits, bias testing, transparency rules, and safety standards for AI systems.
