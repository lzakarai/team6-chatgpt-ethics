Ethical Lenses

While ChatGPT has some potential downsides, different perspectives may alter how we perceive technology. It’s important to consider that ChatGPT needs to earn the trust of users to receive growth in the long term.

Risk Acceptance

From this lens, ChatGPT is viewed as a useful tool even though it has risks. The benefits, such as productivity and access to information, may outweigh the possible harms when users understand the limits of the technology.

Information Consent

This lens focuses on privacy and user control. People should know how their data is collected, stored, and used. Users should also have the ability to make informed choices about whether they want to share information with AI systems.

High Standards

This lens focuses on responsibility, safety, and accountability. Developers and organizations should create clear rules for accuracy, transparency, privacy protection, and fair use of AI.

Social Implications

Despite ChatGPT’s many successes, there are several concerns that need to be addressed. When used in school/college, ChatGPT is a useful resource to help students get through difficult concepts in any class. However, students started using ChatGPT to do their schoolwork for them instead of using it for educational assistance. What would've taken an hour of work, ChatGPT instantly reduces that workload in seconds. Students miss out on learning the necessary soft and technical skills needed in most jobs which hinders their abilities and the integrity of the institution. Since ChatGPT is collecting information based on the internet, it's prone to misinformation. If ChatGPT is being fed large amounts of information from irreputable sources on a certain topic, ChatGPT will eventually convince itself that information is true, leading to inaccuracies. When used in medical procedures, the slightest error can be fatal to patients undergoing surgery and spreading wrong information about people online can lead to multiple cases of defamation. All of these hinder the credibility of the AI which causes huge amounts of mistrust for people using it.

In politics, ChatGPT is very appealing for its wide amount of data and user information. If certain people get a hold of it, they have the ability to control the narrative of events happening and influence user behaviors in their favor. Want to win an election? Use the data by ChatGPT to determine voter needs and concerns. Did a very damaging international crisis happen? Let ChatGPT construe what actually happened to protect the reputation of those behind it. Privacy will be a pretentious issue for users affected because information is valuable in making informed decisions.

For anyone who is frequently active on the internet, you might’ve heard of the term “AI Slop”. This is to refer to AI content used in creative works due to its low quality. Creativity stems from large amounts of passion and strong feelings that people can relate to but AI can’t replicate those types of emotions. For instance, if a business wanted to create a logo for their company, an artist can help accurately represent the company’s brand and ideals within seconds because customers can associate feelings based on the colors and shapes used. Although ChatGPT can create appealing images, it only knows basic designs and doesn’t put enough thought into addressing the needs of the target audience which leads the business to be viewed as lazy or uninteresting.
